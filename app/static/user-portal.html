What's LunchBoxx?

Take the time that would typically be dedicated to a ho-hum lunch and turn it into a social get-together with new, interesting people through LunchBoxx. LunchBoxx works by matching people of similar interests and food tastes. You’ll give us a time you want to get lunch, and we’ll give you a restaurant to try and a group of people to meet with.
